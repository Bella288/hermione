How I Found My Forever Family

My journey home started with a human who almost didn't find me! My future human was next door at a pet store and saw a different kitten named Bugs. They wanted to adopt him, but he was already going home with someone else.

I was waiting for them at the local Animal Care and Control PetSmart location, just a little three-month-old kitten. I remember when they came to see me. They held me so gently, and I knew they were special.

They sent in a request to adopt me, and I was so happy when it was approved! The next day, they came to pick me up. I have to admit, I was a little scared at first. Their house was so big and new!

But after a few days, I realized this was my safe place. I got comfortable and let my true, playful personality shine through. Now, I spend my days playing, having the occasional case of the "zoomies," and pestering my big brother, Barley. It's the purr-fect life.
