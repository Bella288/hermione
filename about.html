How We got Hermione

One day, after my hair appointment, I went over to the pet store nextdoor. I went in and saw a kitten, and ran back to tell my mom.

My mom came to see the cat, and we wanted to adopt him (his name was Bugs). It turned out that somebody adopted him already.

So, we looked on our local Animal Care and Control website, and we found Hermione. So, we went to the PetSmart

where she was located, and we got to hold her. We sent in a request to adopt her, and we got the approval!

The next day, we finally got to pick her up. She was only 3 months old at that point. When we brought

her home, she was very scared. Thankfully, she got used to her new home after a few days. Then, she became

her playful self. She now spends her time playing, going crazy, and pestering her brother, Barley.
